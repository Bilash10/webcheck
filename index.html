Welcome to Elementary Education Department, Nalbari

This website is a dedicated platform to share updates, initiatives, documents, and activities of the Elementary Education Department, Nalbari. We strive to keep stakeholders informed and engaged in the mission of improving education at the elementary level across the district.

Note from the District Elementary Education Officer (DEEO), Nalbari

I am pleased to welcome you to the official website of the Elementary Education Department, Nalbari. Our department is committed to ensuring quality elementary education for every child in the district, with a strong emphasis on inclusivity, innovation, and community participation.

Through this portal, we aim to facilitate transparency, share best practices, and highlight the efforts taken by schools, teachers, and officers. Your suggestions and feedback are always welcome as we work together towards shaping a better future for our children.

— DEEO, Nalbari

About the Department

The Elementary Education Department, Nalbari, works under the Government of Assam to provide inclusive, equitable, and quality education to children in the district. The department oversees government and aided elementary schools, implements national and state-level education schemes, monitors learning outcomes, and promotes innovative teaching practices.

Key Initiatives

  • School Library Programme
  • Handwritten Magazine Competition
  • Mid-Day Meal Scheme Implementation
  • Teacher Training and Capacity Building
  • Monitoring of Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN)
  • Community Mobilization and Parental Engagement
